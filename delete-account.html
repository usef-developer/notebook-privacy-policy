Notebook - Account Deletion

Notebook allows users to permanently delete their account and associated account data.

Delete Your Account From the App

To permanently delete your Notebook account:

  1. Open the Notebook app.
  2. Sign in to the Google account associated with your Notebook account.
  3. Open the app settings.
  4. Go to profile page
  5. Select Delete Account.
  6. Confirm that you want to permanently delete your account.

Data That Is Deleted

When you delete your Notebook account, your Notebook account and associated account data stored by Notebook are permanently deleted, including account information and cloud data associated with your account.

Data Stored on Your Device or Google Drive

Data stored locally on your device may remain until you clear the app's storage or uninstall the app. Backups stored in your personal Google Drive account may also remain and can be deleted manually from your Google Drive.

Subscriptions

Deleting your Notebook account does not automatically cancel an active Google Play subscription. You can manage or cancel your subscription through the Google Play Store.

Request Account Deletion

If you cannot access the Notebook app and would like to request deletion of your account and associated data, please contact us using the developer contact information provided on the Notebook Google Play Store listing.
